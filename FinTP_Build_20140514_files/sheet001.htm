Instructiuni instalare
Pas Instructiune Detalii
1 Se instaelaaza Apache Tomcat 7 (ultima versiune stabila)
2 Se downloadeaza buildurile de la adresele http://fintp:8080/job/CE_CentOS6.4_FinTP_api/ , respectiv http://fintp:8080/job/EE_CentOS6.4_FinTP_ui/
3 Se adauga in fisierul <unde_e_instalat_tomcat>/server.xml liniile urmatoare, inauntrul elementului Context:  De exemplu, deasupra liniei </Context>
<Resource auth="Container" driverClassName="org.postgresql.Driver"
  maxActive="20" maxIdle="10" maxWait="-1" name="jdbc/toFintpConfig"
  password="parola_user_fincfg" type="javax.sql.DataSource" url="url_conexiunne_fincfg"
  username="user_fincfg" />

 <Resource auth="Container" driverClassName="org.postgresql.Driver"
  maxActive="20" maxIdle="10" maxWait="-1" name="jdbc/toFintpData"
  password="parola_user_findata" type="javax.sql.DataSource" url="url_conexiunne_data"
  username="user_findata" />		 Exemplu: url_conexiunne_fincfg = jdbc:postgresql://193.1.1.165:5432/fintpce, user_fincfg = finuiuser;  parola_user_fincfg =  fincfg
4 Se deploy-aza arhivele (.war) pe serverul de Aplicatii Tomcat instalat
